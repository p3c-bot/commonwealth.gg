Congratulations on Recieving your first P3C!

Thank you for your support. You now own a portion of P3C - A core part of the Planetary Prosperity Project. P3C is a decentralized insurance contract that uses the power of an open blockchain to provide an alternative to traditional centralized pension programs. To learn more, go to our home page.

1. Your P3C is safely in an account growing and compounding. No further action is required. Just keep the private key and seed phrase safe.
2. To use the P3C dashboard and manage your holdings, your first step is to install Saturn Wallet.

3. Once Saturn is installed, import the account with the seed phrase from your email, and set a password. Change the network to "ETC Main Network"
5. Now you can navigate to P3C.io/use.html to manage your holdings.
6. Your P3C is always liquid, if you need to sell for fiat currency, you can use the sell button, ETC will be deposited to your Saturn Wallet. This can be sold on an exchange like Coinbase for local fiat currency.
