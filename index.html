Why save with Commonwealth?

  • Transparent: Control and audit your savings at all times. Middlemen can't tamper with funds, or prevent access.
  • Open: Fairly leverages micro-dividends generated from deposits and withdrawls of ETC. All money stays between users!
  • Liquid: Deposit or withdraw easily any time. The smart contract works with any amount.

The story of Ethereum Classic, the blockchain we use

How are Dividends Paid?

  • A 10% dividend is paid out proportionally, in ETC, to all WLTH holders whenever someone buys or sells.
  • The more WLTH are bought or sold, the more dividends are generated.
  • Every WLTH bought automatically increases the price of the next, each one sold decreases the price.
